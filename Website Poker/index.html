Poker Rules

Poker is a card game that combines strategy, skill, and luck. Here are the basic rules:

  • Each player is dealt two cards face down.
  • Players make bets or fold based on their hand.
  • Community cards are dealt in three stages: the flop, the turn, and the river.
  • The goal is to create the best hand or bluff to make others fold.
